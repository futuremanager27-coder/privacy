How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your account at any time using one of the following methods: • In-app: Go to Settings → Delete Account directly within Future Manager 26. • By email: Send a deletion request to contactfuturemanager@gmail.com from the address associated with your account. We will process your request as soon as possible and no later than 30 days after receiving your request.

What Gets Deleted

Deleting your account permanently and irreversibly removes the following data: • Your Firebase Authentication account (email, login credentials). • Your cloud save (Firestore): career progress, squad, finances, season stats, and all game data. • Your Coin balance and any associated in-game currency. • Your game progression and all data linked to your account.

⚠ This action is permanent and cannot be undone. Lost data cannot be recovered.

Data Retention After Deletion

Unless required by applicable law, no personal account data is retained after your account has been deleted.

Local Data on Your Device

Data stored locally on your device can be removed by: • Uninstalling the Future Manager 26 application, or • Clearing the app's storage from your Android device settings (Settings → Apps → Future Manager 26 → Storage → Clear Data).
